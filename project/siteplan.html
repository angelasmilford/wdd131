I chose this title because my site will be covering each island nation or archipelago in the Micronesian region

Home Islands Culture Tourism

Site Purpose

The website is going to be informative site about the Micronesian islands of the Pacific region. It also aims to promote tourism among these islands. The navigation panel of the homepage will include Home, Islands, Culture or History, and Tourism. The content in the homepage will hold a responsive hero image, a welcome message overlay on the hero image, and a footer that shows the author of the site and the time the site was published.
