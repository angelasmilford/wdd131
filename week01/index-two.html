Pago Pago, American Samoa

American Samoa is an unincorporated U.S. territory that consists of the eastern islands of the Samoan archipelago. There are seven islands and atolls that make up American Samoa: Tutuila, Aunu'u, Ofu, Olosega, Ta'u, Rose Atoll, and Swains Island. Of the seven islands, Tutuila is the American Samoa's main island due to its notable size. The capital of this island nation is Pago Pago.
